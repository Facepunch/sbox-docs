# What is s&box?

Welcome to s&box! If you are a player wondering what exactly this game is, you are in the right place.

s&box (pronounced "sandbox") is not a single, traditional video game. Instead, it is a **platform** and a **game engine**. It is a place where you can discover, play, and share thousands of different games created by the community.

## How It Works

When you launch s&box, you are greeted by the Main Menu, which acts as a browser. From here, you can explore a massive variety of games—from fast-paced shooters and racing games to relaxing puzzle games and role-playing servers. 

When you find a game that looks fun and click to join it:
1.  **Automatic Downloads:** s&box automatically downloads all the necessary files (maps, models, and logic) to run that specific game.
2.  **Seamless Transition:** Once the download finishes, you are placed directly into the game.
3.  **No Subscriptions Required:** Unlike some older games, you do not need to manually manage "Workshop subscriptions" or figure out which addons to install. The platform handles everything for you behind the scenes.

## What Can I Play?

Because s&box provides powerful tools to its creators, the types of games you can play are virtually limitless. You might find yourself:
- Exploring a custom recreation of a classic board game.
- Teaming up with friends in a cooperative survival map.
- Competing in a fully-featured arena shooter.
- Hanging out in a social hub or a virtual cinema.

## The Citizen Avatar

Many games in s&box use a shared, customizable character model known as the **Citizen**. 

From the Main Menu, you can open the Avatar Editor to customize your Citizen's appearance. You can change their clothing, hats, body shape, and colors. Whenever you join a game that uses the standard Citizen model, your custom appearance will automatically carry over. However, some games may choose to override your avatar to fit their specific theme or gameplay rules.
