# Finding Games & Addons

s&box is a platform containing thousands of community-created games, addons, and maps. This guide explains how to find and play them.

## The Main Menu

When you launch s&box, the primary way to find content is through the in-game Main Menu browser. This is your central hub for discovering new experiences.

1. Click the **Play** tab.
2. You will see a list of featured, trending, and newly updated games.
3. Use the search bar or tags to find specific game modes or genres (e.g., "FPS", "Sandbox", "Racing").

When you select a game, you will see its details page. From here, you have two primary ways to play:

*   **Quick Play:** This immediately connects you to an active server or creates a new one for you in the background. It's the fastest way to jump into the action.
*   **Server Browser:** This lets you view a list of all currently active public servers running that specific game. You can see how many players are in each server and pick one that suits you.

## How Addons Work

You **do not** need to manually download or subscribe to content before playing.

When you click "Join" on a server or "Play" on a game mode, s&box will automatically download all required files (the game's code, custom maps, models, and sounds) in the background.

> **Roblox/GMod Comparison:** Similar to Roblox or Garry's Mod, the game fetches everything needed for that specific session behind the scenes. You just click and wait for the loading screen.

## Managing Downloaded Content

Because the engine automatically downloads files for every game you join, your disk usage can increase significantly over time.

### Clearing Cache

If you want to free up space from games you no longer play:

1. Open the **Settings** menu.
2. Navigate to the **Storage** or **Cache** section.
3. Click **Clear Cache** to remove downloaded addons.
4. *Note: The next time you join a server, you will have to redownload its files.*

## Hosting Your Own Game

If you want to play privately with friends or have control over the server settings:

*   **Create Game:** When on a game's details page, look for the option to create your own game.
*   **Visibility:** You can usually choose to make the server public (anyone can join) or private (invite only or friends-only).

## Troubleshooting Downloads

:::warning Download Stalling
If you get stuck on a "Downloading Addons" screen:
1. Ensure your firewall isn't blocking s&box.
2. Cancel the join and try again. Sometimes the download connection drops.
3. If it persists, try clearing your cache via the settings menu.
:::
