# Account & Steam Setup

This guide explains how your s&box account works, how it links with Steam, and what information is shared when you play games.

## Steam Requirement

s&box requires a valid Steam account to play. There is no separate "s&box account" or a secondary launcher you need to sign into. Your identity on the platform is directly tied to your Steam profile.

When you launch s&box:
* You are automatically logged in using the active Steam account on your computer.
* Your display name in all games will match your current Steam profile name.
* Your Steam avatar (profile picture) is used in menus, scoreboards, and social hubs.

## Account Linking & Progression

Because s&box uses your Steam ID (a unique number associated with your Steam account), your progress is saved across different games and servers automatically.

* **Game Data:** If you play a role-playing game and earn currency, that data is tied to your Steam ID. When you reconnect the next day, the game server recognizes your Steam ID and loads your data.
* **Bans & Moderation:** If you break the rules on a community server and are banned, that ban is applied to your Steam ID.

## Privacy & Avatars

s&box features a global **Avatar Editor** in the Main Menu, allowing you to customize a "Citizen" character with different clothing, accessories, and body types.

* **Global Appearance:** This customized avatar is saved to your account and is used automatically in any game that supports the standard Citizen models.
* **Game-Specific Overrides:** While you have a global avatar, individual game creators have the power to override your appearance. For example, a fantasy game might force you to play as a knight or a goblin, ignoring your customized Citizen avatar entirely. This is up to the game's developer.

## Disconnecting or Changing Accounts

If you need to change accounts (for example, if you share a PC):
1. Close s&box.
2. Log out of Steam and log into the other account.
3. Relaunch s&box.

Your progress and customized avatars are separate for each Steam account, even if they are played on the same computer.
